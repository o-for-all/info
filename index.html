O-Experiences

based on almost true events

My first experience with O? Well I needed to buy a new laptop a few months ago, after my old one just fell apart. While I was researching and looking for reviews I saw O with like MacBook Pro X - the most sustainable powerhouse as headline. I remembered a friend of mine told me about O and that its a sustainable marketplace or something. But I very much doubted that the MacBook Pro can be sustainable in any way. So of course I went on the O-Marketplace and I saw all this in depth information about how Apple asks you to send in your old Apple products so they can recycle them or dispose of them appropriately and what not. I mean it was way better explained by the other people there. Anyway I didnt buy the laptop, because I need Windows on my machine. But I saw an attached article comparing all the latest laptops similar to the MacBook Pro X. Turns out Dell basically does the same thing as Apple and was only 3 points less sustainable from 100 points. So from there it was quite easy to choose the manufacturer and the article also pointed out which model is best for which use case. Since I only use my laptop to write and watch Netflix I didnt actually need a powerhouse. But the best part was, one month after I bought my laptop I received a message that I got 51 Euros as credit. Turns out because the laptop was 70% sustainable and was like 980 Euro I got part of the profits back for buying more sustainably. I mean O is amazing. There is so much more to it, but it's best to just discover it yourself. It opened up a new world for me.
